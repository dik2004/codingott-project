# JS V1 - Day 24

## Task
Ek aisa **Kids Learning Project** banaiye jisme **A to Z** tak saare alphabets show ho.

---

## Alphabet Display
- Screen par **A to Z** alphabets buttons/cards ke form me show hone chahiye.
- User kisi bhi alphabet par click kare.

---

## Full Form Show
Alphabet par click karne ke baad uska full form show hona chahiye, jaise:

- **A for Apple**
- **B for Ball**
- **C for Cat**

---

## Audio Feature
- Har alphabet ke liye ek audio file honi chahiye.
- Alphabet par click karne par uska audio automatically play hona chahiye.

---

## Auto Hide Feature
- Full form show hone ke baad **automatic kuch seconds (ex: 3-5 sec)** ke baad hide bhi ho jana chahiye.

---

## Mic Icon Enable / Disable
- Jab audio play ho raha ho tab **Mic Icon enable (active)** ho jana chahiye.
- Jab audio complete ho jaye tab **Mic Icon disabled (inactive)** ho jana chahiye.

---

## Important Instruction
Is project ki saari functionality **JavaScript** se implement karni hai.
